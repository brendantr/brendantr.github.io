Productivity meets learning

Simulisten is designed for students, professionals, and lifelong learners who want to make the most of their study time. Combine audiobooks, lectures, or podcasts with ambient focus music to create the perfect learning environment.

Works completely offline once your content is loaded, making it ideal for commutes, study sessions, or anywhere you need to focus without distractions. No internet required, no data tracking—just you, your learning content, and your focus music.
