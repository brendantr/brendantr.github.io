Simulisten is an offline-friendly iOS app that combines learning content with background music through dual-track audio playback—perfect for audiobooks, podcasts, and focused study sessions.

Powerful dual-track playback

Everything you need for productive learning sessions with personalized audio control.

Dual playback system

Two independent audio players for learning content and background music—play simultaneously or separately with dedicated controls.

Independent volume control

Separate volume sliders for each track let you perfectly balance spoken content and music to match your focus needs.

Background playback

Audio continues seamlessly even when the app is in the background or your device is locked—perfect for uninterrupted sessions.

Tab-based navigation

Modern SwiftUI design with four intuitive sections: Listen Now, Library, Profile, and Settings—with light and dark mode support.

Built for your learning flow

Whether you're listening to audiobooks, podcasts, or presentations, Simulisten helps you stay focused with customizable background music that adapts to your study style.

Our offline-friendly app supports local audio files only, giving you complete control over your productivity and focus sessions—no subscriptions, no streaming, just your content the way you want it.
